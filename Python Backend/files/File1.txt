PSNA College of Engineering and Technology, also known as PSNACET, is a Autonomous Institution which is located in Dindigul.

There are various Departments available in PSNACET. Some of them include,
1. Computer Science and Engineering
2. Electronics and Communication Engineering
3. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering
5. Information Technology
6. Civil Engineering

To get into each department, The student has to have a minimum amount of Cut-off mark from their 12th grade.
Here are some required cut-off mark for each departments:
1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) - required: 170, recommended: 180
2. Electronics and Communication Engineering - required: 150, recommended: 160
3. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science - required: 170, recommended: 185
4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering - required: 120, recommended: 140
5. Information Technology - required: 160, recommended: 170
6. Civil Engineering - required: 100, recommended: 120
